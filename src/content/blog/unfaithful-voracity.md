<p style="text-align: left">I don’t fall for that typical love that attracts bloodshot eyes.</p>
<p style="text-align: left">Encapsulated in a pulsing red is a desire that repels me.</p>
<p style="text-align: left">For with every beat of a heart, there is one in sync with mine.</p>
<p style="text-align: left">Somewhere.</p>
<p style="text-align: left">And in whose chest I see no wall deterring me.</p>
<p style="text-align: left">Of frivolous passion and soft ashen fears</p>
<p style="text-align: left">That fell to the water and melted like liquid fire,</p>
<p style="text-align: left"><em>We are rare</em>.</p>
<p style="text-align: left">I listen for the dull thump that echoes over mountains.</p>
<p style="text-align: left">There is none too honest and whole</p>
<p style="text-align: left">That does not attract my quivering eyes.</p>
<p style="text-align: left">Beware:</p>
<p style="text-align: left">Typical love is that vomit-inducing kind of transient that sifts</p>
<p style="text-align: left">Through skeletal fingers, hungering for distasteful appetite.</p>
<p style="text-align: left">Tunes reverb in flushed ears and I am merry</p>
<p style="text-align: left">And I happily bleed out the sharp air.</p>
<p style="text-align: left">That typical love that excites a million and more of starving souls?</p>
<p style="text-align: left">Its voluptuous and tasty exterior is all addictive flavor.</p>
<p style="text-align: left">My tongue craves only a hint of what it can give me.</p>
<p style="text-align: left">So there.</p>
