<p class="font_8">LOVE,

A mystery no one can't quite describe,
a feeling that seems to strive,
in young star-crossed lovers eyes.

An emotion once embedded in a persons soul,
runs rampant and out of control.
Resistance, futile, it overpowers all other limits and goals.

A song oh' so sweet,
it makes the humming birds weep.
It strives, prospers, turns the world inside out,
makes you want to scream and shout.

Then finally,

A moment so magical,
it lasts a lifetime.
For nothing makes the heart feel so fine,
As a KISS for the first time.</p>
<p class="font_8"></p>
<p class="font_8"><em>by Ryan Smith, 15, John F. Kennedy School, Berlin ,Germany</em></p>
