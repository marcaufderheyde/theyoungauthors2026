A really good day – a poem filled with appreciation about the smaller things part of the human experience</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>My head feels clear, maybe it’s the air, maybe it’s just me,<br>But every ray of light around me points to something I wish to see, <br>Every sense that tingles seems to smile with pleasure,<br>Everything about today is a long-forgotten treasure,<br>And everything I haven’t yet been, is waiting patiently to be seen.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Euphoria, I feel a warmth and a glow so loud, I feel the people that make me proud,<br>I feel the wavelength that I may share, I feel my neighbours loving stare, <br>We are but people, we are one team,<br>A lovely and dysfunctional pillowcase, old and ancient, bursting at the seam.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I smile, for today is just a really good day,<br>Why you may ask, I cannot quite say,<br>But I feel it in my bones, a heaviness so weirdly pleasant,<br>I spread out non-existent wings and feel as glorious as a pheasant.<br>I breathe, funny, such a simple yet elegant thing,<br>The birds flutter, the breeze is light, I open my mouth and start to sing,<br>Oh how lovely, what a day, oh how lovely come what may,<br>I have no worry, I have no fear, my insides are bubbling full of cheer,<br>My eyes start to water as I feel quite so right,<br>These are the days worth every single fight,<br>This day is lovely and sweet and great,<br>This day smiles on me, I realize its fate.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This is a good day, a really good day.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>By Marc Auf der Heyde</em></p>
