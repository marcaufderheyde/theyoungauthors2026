<p class="font_8">Like a ship
on frozen water,
my body
can't move forward

An anchor
holds my mind too close
I can't free myself
from the darkness below

You are the opposite of me-
you and your long-found bravery,
floating fearlessly
on the undpredictable sea

I wish to be touched
by a drop of rain,
so I can let my flowers bloom
whose seeds slumber in vain</p>
<p class="font_8"></p>
<p class="font_8"><em>By Diana Parsons, 17, Anna-Seghers-School, Germany</em></p>
