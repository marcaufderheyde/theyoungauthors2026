<p class="font_8">An endless journey of hope
we travel every day
Man knows not of the outcome
it is so pointless to say

Cradles rock violently to and fro
and stay unfaithfully well kempt
Sacrifice to the moving wave
is a futile attempt

The balance of the unexplainable
keeps us in debt
Try and listen carefully
But have no need to fret
No ear can explain
So just listen, don't place a bet</p>
<p class="font_8"></p>
<p class="font_8"><em>by Charl Moller, 17, Hoerskool Jan Van Riebeeck, South Africa</em></p>
