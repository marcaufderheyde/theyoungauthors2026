<p class="font_8">Her eyes are a chasm</p>
<p class="font_8">Directly to her soul</p>
<p class="font_8">Concealed by a veil of laughter</p>
<p class="font_8">The ringing smiles</p>
<p class="font_8">Of genuine bliss</p>
<p class="font_8">Echo hollowly within.</p>
<p class="font_8">It is either ecstasy or apathy.</p>
<p class="font_8">There is no in between,</p>
<p class="font_8">Because of the pressure</p>
<p class="font_8">The expectations</p>
<p class="font_8">The highhopes doomed</p>
<p class="font_8">to plummet.</p>
<p class="font_8">And so the chasm to her soul</p>
<p class="font_8">Stays undiscovered</p>
<p class="font_8">Unchartered</p>
<p class="font_8">And she tries</p>
<p class="font_8">To pave an entry</p>
<p class="font_8">No one dares break the surface.</p>
<p class="font_8">No one ever dares</p>
<p class="font_8">In this world of momentary pleasures</p>
<p class="font_8">Inhabited by permanent cowards</p>
<p class="font_8">Scared of reality.</p>
<p class="font_8"><em>by Penelope Sars, 17, John F. Kennedy School, Berlin, Germany</em></p>
