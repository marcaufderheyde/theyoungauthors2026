<p class="font_8">I must be a great man,</p>
<p class="font_8">For you to care about me,</p>
<p class="font_8"></p>
<p class="font_8">You are champagne, my love,</p>
<p class="font_8">You are rainbow-parrot-free,</p>
<p class="font_8"></p>
<p class="font_8">The drum of life thumps twice,</p>
<p class="font_8">With you I feel it three,</p>
<p class="font_8"></p>
<p class="font_8">For you I'm sky-stars-cloud,</p>
<p class="font_8">So you can fly through me.</p>
<p class="font_8"></p>
<p class="font_8"><em>Sam Wasserman, 16, Babelsberger Filmgymnasium, Berlin, Germany</em></p>
<p class="font_8"></p>
