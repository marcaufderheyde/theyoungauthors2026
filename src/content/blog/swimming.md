<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I began as a little fish in a little pond.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>As I discovered new things,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>learned new tricks,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I grew.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Soon I became the biggest fish in that little pond</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>And the only thing I found was that I needed more space.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>So I began my journey down the river.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>As I swam&nbsp;</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>the river only got wider,&nbsp;</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>and the other fish around me were much bigger.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I kept swimming&nbsp;</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>So I could be as big as them.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Of course</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I made some mistakes along the way</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>When I thought of myself&nbsp;</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>As the biggest fish from that&nbsp;</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Small little pond,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>But the river didn’t care who I was.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>It took me where I didn’t want to go </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>When I wasn’t paying attention to it.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I found that it was harder to swim</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>When I was facing backwards,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>against the current.&nbsp;</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>So I learned from my mistakes</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>And the river taught me more tricks,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Showed me new discoveries,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Until I became one of the biggest fish in the river. <br></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>As I swim,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>And as I grow,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The river widens more.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>And I’m discovering that&nbsp;</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>All the other big fish are heading down</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A similar current.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Soon we will find ourselves at the mouth of the ocean,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Where we will be little fish once more.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Some of us may ride its waves,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Some may fight its currents,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>But no matter what we do,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>We must keep swimming.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Someday,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I hope,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>When I grow tired of my space in the ocean,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Perhaps I will be able&nbsp;</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>to walk on land.&nbsp;</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
