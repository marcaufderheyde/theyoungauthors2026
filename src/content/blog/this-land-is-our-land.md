<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>

This land is our land</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This land is our land, is what they say,<br>This land is to share, without sacrifice or pay,<br>So why does this land, feel tortured and burnt<br>This land seems to weep with sorrow and hurt,<br>This land of ours, oh how we hurt.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I see so many smiles, so many embraceful cheers,<br>They make me want to smile as well, stretch my face from ear to ear,<br>I want to share the love, if only my conviction were real,<br>For as I look at the faces around me, Joy is not that which I feel.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The soil beneath my feet cries and sighs, <br>Heavy on it's back are deceit and lies,<br>But I cannot force open the eyes of those around me,<br>I cannot paint a striking picture of the truths that seem to be,<br>All I can do but mourn, is be so hopeful and aware,<br>And try to pry my eyes away from the sorrows that attract my stare.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I breathe in and I breathe out, <br>My tears wet my cheek, my heart weighs heavy with bitter doubt,<br>I see no brightness for this land, I see no glittering rays of sun,<br>All I want to do is forget, all I want to do is run,<br>But that we may not, for that lie is bigger,<br>So do not forget, do not pull the trigger.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>We stay aware, pained and in debt.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>by Marc Auf der Heyde</em></p>
