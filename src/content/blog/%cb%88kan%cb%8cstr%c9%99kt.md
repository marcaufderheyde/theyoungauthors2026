Straight lines do not make straight thoughts.

And so to bend the rules is fitting.

Clear sounds formulate dissonant ideas.

And so to stretch the note is soothing.

Monotone settings do not make simple senses.

And so to color the gray is common.

Chaotic choruses do not concoct harmonic voices.

And so to speak superiorly is special.

Ordinary construct does not inspire order.

And so to express the abstract is glorifying.
