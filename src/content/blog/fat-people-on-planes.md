Fat people on planes</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Oh I'm sorry, did you expect me to say large boned? <br>Did you expect me to excuse each excess stone, <br>Would you be able to ignore the layer of fat the envelopes my phone?<br>No. If you're fat, pay for business class,<br>Why must I be punished for your fat arse? <br>I can regulate my eating, you cannot, <br>I'm not saying fat people should be shot,<br>They just shouldn't fly in economy.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Don't lie, I've got a point, their stench of oozing grease assaults my senses,<br>I try to sleep, to ignore the pound of meat that pushes against my side, threatens to break down my fences,<br>But alas, to no avail, this oaf does snore as well, how swell, my dream comes true,<br>My eyes wonder longingly for any source of help, but I cannot lock eyes with a member of the crew,<br>I am alone, to fight off this beast, to save my seat, my greatest feat,<br>But what can I do but sit and pray, or do I play a beggars game?</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I slowly turn my face to it, <br>To face this goon the little shit,<br>I give him my best intense eyes, <br>A mirror image of when he spies fries,<br>And glare my best German look of disapproval, <br>I thrust from my wavelength, signals of removal,<br>But dare I say, I'm shocked to see, he is unmoved,<br>He returns an equal look for free.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I do this several times, again and again,<br>I realize this display is why the world hates men,<br>And yet I can't stop, I must correct this fat slop,<br>For when I book a seat on a flight, <br>If you're black or white, small or large of height, a pretty or an ugly sight, <br>I care only if you are fat. And if you're fat, shame on you, <br>Heed my warning, cut down on the stew, <br>I don't want to hate you, but you force my hand,<br>You are quite truly a very fat man,<br>You ruined my flight, you stole from me my sleep, <br>And I hope that karma gets you for it, and shears the fat from you like wool from a sheep.</p>
