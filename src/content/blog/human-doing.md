<span style="font-weight: 400">The hindrance of one’s natural affairs </span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Sparks the inevitability of heartache. </span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">And any effort to suspend this kind of misery</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Is a downward spiral of emotional wake. </span>

&nbsp;

<span style="font-weight: 400">Where success is marked by capital accumulation,</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">We all must take the same shallow path. </span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Some are cast off by means of minor discrepancies </span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">And few emerge victoriously from the aftermath. </span>

&nbsp;

<span style="font-weight: 400">To shed the rotting integument</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">That suffocates and shrivels all empathy</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Is the option that best disposes the rest </span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Of the poisonous orthodoxies here presently. </span>

&nbsp;

<span style="font-weight: 400">We fit our grand title no longer</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">As our one existential question dissolves</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Among the mayhem and mischief we cause</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">To earn a buck rather than evolve.</span>
