<p class="font_8">We have no wings, to fly and soar,</p>
<p class="font_8">we cannot escape, when our heart is sore,</p>
<p class="font_8">what we have is emotion, which cuts down the deepest,</p>
<p class="font_8">one cut, two cut, I've lost count of how many cuts,</p>
<p class="font_8">the mountain that is human nature, reigns high among the clouds so white,</p>
<p class="font_8">it has always been and will be the steepest.</p>
<p class="font_8">What makes us the strongest, makes us the weakest,</p>
<p class="font_8">A man who feels nothing has conquered immortality,</p>
<p class="font_8">A boy who feels nothing is destined for greatness,</p>
<p class="font_8">Feelings like those that feast in our soul,</p>
<p class="font_8">Make us the meekest and swallow us whole.</p>
<p class="font_8"></p>
<p class="font_8">So why do I feel,</p>
<p class="font_8">If I am one of the few who sees all the flaws,</p>
<p class="font_8">The dangers and troubles that bite at our heels,</p>
<p class="font_8">To consume us and drive us into their jaws.</p>
<p class="font_8"></p>
<p class="font_8">We consider those who do not feel,</p>
<p class="font_8">Vermin,</p>
<p class="font_8">Clinically insane,</p>
<p class="font_8">All those dreaded people that force us to double over, wretch and reel.</p>
<p class="font_8">But it’s simple, it’s because we are afraid of the power they know,</p>
<p class="font_8">They are not slaves of sorrow, not fools of love,</p>
<p class="font_8">They have the power we yearn, to fly away like a dove,</p>
<p class="font_8">And be at peace for they do not feel.</p>
<p class="font_8"></p>
<p class="font_8">But I feel,</p>
<p class="font_8">I choose to feel, despite it all,</p>
<p class="font_8">Every feeling that brushes my mind,</p>
<p class="font_8">I take it as a blessing, a new building block that I am to find,</p>
<p class="font_8">Whether it is love, the deepest cut of all those cuts,</p>
<p class="font_8">Whether it is the mockery from someone,</p>
<p class="font_8">a cut so small can I even call it a cut?</p>
<p class="font_8">Or whether it is the sorrow, the loss of someone dear to my heart,</p>
<p class="font_8">The feeling that I cannot go on, that I and her have finally part,</p>
<p class="font_8">The tremor I feel, the quakes that course through me,</p>
<p class="font_8">All part of a feeling, Which helps to build me.</p>
<p class="font_8">I appreciate every feeling,</p>
<p class="font_8">whether tears run down my cheeks, for she is not there,</p>
<p class="font_8">Whether my feet are tired, whether I am gasping for air,</p>
<p class="font_8">She is not there, I looked again, we will never be a pair.</p>
<p class="font_8"></p>
<p class="font_8">A memory that will never ever escape, she leaves my world wide open and obliviously agape.</p>
<p class="font_8"></p>
<p class="font_8">I am an appreciative person, there is no shame in that,</p>
<p class="font_8">But expression has never exactly been my forte,</p>
<p class="font_8">In fact to be honest, It’s a little bit flat,</p>
<p class="font_8">No bubbles like glass, wafting up,</p>
<p class="font_8">An empty glass, an empty cup,</p>
<p class="font_8">But through words I can try,</p>
<p class="font_8">If ever only try to say what I think.</p>
<p class="font_8"></p>
<p class="font_8">You are unusually and unearthly, beautiful for lack of a better word,</p>
<p class="font_8">The way you choose to go about things,</p>
<p class="font_8">to say I’ve seen it somewhere before would be a lie,</p>
<p class="font_8">it would be nothing short of bloody absurd.</p>
<p class="font_8">Your personality is strong like the blade of a sword,</p>
<p class="font_8">Read to strike and defend if need be,</p>
<p class="font_8">And one day it will strike as you sail across the sea,</p>
<p class="font_8">And take the world by storm, you need only perform one chord.</p>
<p class="font_8"></p>
<p class="font_8">I thoroughly enjoyed acting with a spirit such as yours,</p>
<p class="font_8">Flowing like the river and wetting the blades of grass,</p>
<p class="font_8">Your complete control over what will occur,</p>
<p class="font_8">The act whizzes by in only a blur.</p>
<p class="font_8">It is not a play, it is not someone else.</p>
<p class="font_8"></p>
<p class="font_8">You carve your own future,</p>
<p class="font_8">You choose to feel and not feel yet,</p>
<p class="font_8">As though it were actually an option,</p>
<p class="font_8">And on you never let.</p>
<p class="font_8">Inconspicuous, you say you like mysteries,</p>
<p class="font_8">Ironic only in the sense that you are the greatest mystery you yourself know,</p>
<p class="font_8">And the longer you live the mystery will grow, until one day,</p>
<p class="font_8">One day,</p>
<p class="font_8">You will suddenly understand.</p>
<p class="font_8"></p>
<p class="font_8"><em>by Paul Gracey, 17, Westerford High School, Cape Town, South Africa</em></p>
