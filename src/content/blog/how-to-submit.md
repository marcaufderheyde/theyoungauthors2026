<ol>
 	<li>Create an account by clicking on the login button and then the register button. Make sure you fill out all your details correctly, then check your email to activate your account!</li>
</ol>

<h4></h4>
2. Once your account has been created and verified, login by using the Log In button as depicted above.

3. Once you are logged in, you will be taken to your theyoungauthors dashboard, as seen below. Once on the dashboard, click the 'New' Tab in the toolbar at the top of the page and select 'New Post', as seen below.

<img class="size-full wp-image-657 aligncenter" src="/images/2016/04/Screenshot-6.png" alt="Screenshot (6)" width="1073" height="607" />

&nbsp;

4. You are now on the screen where you can directly submit your work. Fill out the necessary information, Title, Content, Tags, Category and featured image. Any inappropriate content will be filtered out automatically and removed.

<img class="wp-image-660 aligncenter" src="/images/2016/04/Screenshot-13.png" alt="Screenshot (13)" width="1018" height="512" />

5. Before publishing, please make sure to credit your work in the following format: <em>By (NAME), (AGE), (SCHOOL), (COUNTRY)</em>

6. If you like theyoungauthors, please consider donating so that we can keep the website active and continue expanding internationally!
