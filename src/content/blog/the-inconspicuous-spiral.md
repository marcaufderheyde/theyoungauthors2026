<p class="font_8">The Inconspicuous Spiral of the ticking tocking time bomb.</p>
<p class="font_8">The world is quite harsh, in all things in place,</p>
<p class="font_8">An Arabic group of terror, who ‘do it for grace’.</p>
<p class="font_8">Such awful events, the negativity they spread,</p>
<p class="font_8">Weighs the good vibes beneath the course rough waves,</p>
<p class="font_8">With hearts full of lead, it had to be said.</p>
<p class="font_8">The trees are trying their best to stand tall,</p>
<p class="font_8">Every new bud they grow, has a tendency to fall,</p>
<p class="font_8">The colors are blank, like the pages of my mind,</p>
<p class="font_8">Every sharp corner turn, I know not what I will find.</p>
<p class="font_8">What darkness will emerge, or illumination I be drowned in,</p>
<p class="font_8">Where the exit is, puzzles makes me painstakingly thickheaded,</p>
<p class="font_8"> Pale as I choose with randomness to my curves,</p>
<p class="font_8">Ignoring the obvious things that I have dreaded.</p>
<p class="font_8">The world is an ocean, a flow of knowledge which swims in itself,</p>
<p class="font_8">How can one make it, how can one even hope to simply reach the top shelf.</p>
<p class="font_8">It matters not, the words of a wise man are the words of one who’s not,</p>
<p class="font_8">All the people who doubt his words,</p>
<p class="font_8">Are those of plainness who will be finished and forgot.</p>
<p class="font_8">From West to South, from Low ground to high,</p>
<p class="font_8">We analyze everything intending to sigh,</p>
<p class="font_8">We know not, what tackles here,</p>
<p class="font_8">What tackles there, we hide in fear,</p>
<p class="font_8">And on our lives, do run in circles.</p>
<p class="font_8">Pi times this and radius times that,</p>
<p class="font_8">The volume of a cone versus the importance of politics</p>
<p class="font_8">And how to shed fat,</p>
<p class="font_8">It’s like they want us to do badly in life,</p>
<p class="font_8">Badly, to take a knife,</p>
<p class="font_8">And delve a little deeper into the flesh of mankind.</p>
<p class="font_8">Those who stare into the abyss of their goal,</p>
<p class="font_8">Are the first ones to wither and tarnish their soul.</p>
<p class="font_8"><em>by Marc Auf der Heyde, 17, John F Kennedy School, Berlin Germany</em></p>
