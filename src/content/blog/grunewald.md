<ul>
 	<li><span style="font-weight: 400">Pearling clear waters</span></li>
</ul>
<span style="font-weight: 400">Slowed and quickened</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">By the gust of</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Cold autumn wind</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Binding the waves </span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">To their earthy grave</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Gated by</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Great Leafy Trees</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Dancing in the silver mirrors</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Of the unruffled waters</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Throughout which</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Long spiraling barks</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Of mellow oaks</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Crowned with yellow</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">And green</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Stand uniform</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">In their earthly halls</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Surrounded by the rare silence</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Of the city of Gray</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400">Grown around it all </span>
