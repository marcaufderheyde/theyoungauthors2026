<p class="font_8">Autumn leaves
and open pages,
weeping trees
and breezes of changes

Skies are dim
and the rain is cold,
but lovers are kissing
and they still believe

In the red of the apple,
the gold of the hay
and the magic
of October days</p>
<p class="font_8"></p>
<p class="font_8"><em>By Diana Parsons, 17, Anna-Seghers-School, Germany</em></p>
