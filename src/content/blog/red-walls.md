<p class="font_9">Anxious screams lie in the air.
People's faces swollen with tears.
Sirens out in the distance.
Police men perplexed.

Friends and relatives screaming.
Big plastic bags with a zipper.
Forensics at the scene,
Taking pictures.

Two guns,
1 knife,
A roll of duct tape,
A hammer.

A man lyng on the floor,
motionless.

Walls.
Painted with blood.
Red walls.</p>
<p class="font_9"></p>
<p class="font_9"><em>By J. Rugger, 16,  John F. Kennedy School, Berlin, Germany</em></p>
