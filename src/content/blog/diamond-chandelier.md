I'm tired and anxious

I hear an ominous whimper

I want to go back to my years when I thought life was simpler.

I had a nightmare last night

so i don't want to go to sleep.

Oh Conscious stop calling! Leave your deprived message after the beep.

Tomorrow morning I leave for the first day of senior year but

Can't I just hang beautifully and tragically from a diamond chandelier?
