<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A bumpy night<br/>By <em>Anonymous</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Twas a bumpy night, late on a Sunday,<br/>The bumping didn't end till early on the Monday,<br/>We bumped to the music, we bumped back and forth,<br/>And when the bumping was finished, each bump did prove it's worth.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I think back to each and every bump, as I slumber away in this new found cage,<br/>Each and every bump was fresh and clean, a brand new book turning a page,<br/>And it's weird that I want to write about these bumps, it's kind of hard to bring a bump to life with words,<br/>But each bump was so alive, it screeched and fluttered like a majestic bird.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>These bumps make me smile, these bumps pump serotonin like no pill could ever,<br/>And as my receptors are slowly depleted, I wonder why I'm at the end of this endeavor,<br/>Like a key that fits it's keyhole with such a grace and ease,<br/>Each bump did something to me as I swatted for invisible flees,<br/>My worries were great, they peaked after the mountainous bump,<br/>And as these worries took me, I wondered if I could regrow this stump,<br/>For my arm had fallen off, and I realized it's uselessness,<br/>But this injury was nothing new, only was the taste of bitterness.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Now that the bumping has finally ended,<br/>I stroke my heart which is not yet mended,<br/>The bumping made me treasure and appreciate, that bumping is a joy,<br/>But the bumping also made me realize, that bumping is no toy.<br/>So I look forward to the next bumps, the next smiles and the next beats,<br/>And with each future bump of mine, I'll try to sweep her off her feet. </p>
