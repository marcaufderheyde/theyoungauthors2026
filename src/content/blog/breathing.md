<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">So breathe and begin!</span></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">Two hundred and seventy</span></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">And then some</span></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">Days spent blind</span></p>
<p class="font_9"></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">But now they are nothing</span></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">Great symphony of sound</span></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">May it mark the start</span></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">Like a gun for a race: scream!</span></p>
<p class="font_9"></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">Great symphony of sound</span></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">A thousand oceans over sand</span></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">Worldly winds flow and work</span></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">Scraping stones. That is the sound</span></p>
<p class="font_9"></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">And that is it’s rough flow</span></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">Of ins and outs</span></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">Through the mouth and nose</span></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">Steadily without pattern</span></p>
<p class="font_9"></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">Which does heave hearts</span></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">Fill fingers with tingles</span></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">Make all want to shout</span></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">Hug, kiss, cry and live!</span></p>
<p class="font_9"></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">Oh great symphony of sound, do you promise not to end</span></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">Do you promise not to go</span></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">Oh great symphony of sound, promise not to leave me</span></p>
<p class="font_9"><span class="color_15">Lying silent and alone.</span></p>
<p class="font_9"></p>
<p class="font_9"><em><span class="color_15">by Isaiah Michalski, 17, Babelsberger Filmgymnasium, Potsdam Germany</span></em></p>
