<p class="font_8">I am a feather drifting from a bird
I am a card swaying from the table
I am the leaves floating during fall
I am all things falling slowly to the surface
And when I land
I will seep into the ground
And let the soil engulf me
And the creatures consume my innards
Until I've become one
With nature
And God
And Myself</p>
<p class="font_8"></p>
<p class="font_8"><em>Alexis Potebnya, 15, lakewood high, USA</em></p>
