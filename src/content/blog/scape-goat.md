<p class="font_8">hate me</p>
<p class="font_8">with all you’ve got
take out every last bit of stress out on me
every worry
every last bit of sadness
it was my fault
scream it out
on the ears that have heard it
day and night
until the ends of emotional exsistence
I’m happy that I can listen
to your sorrows
in form of hate filled screams
being angry is better than being sad, right?
</p>
<p class="font_8"><em>by Eli Goodman, 15, John F. Kennedy School, Berlin Germany</em></p>
