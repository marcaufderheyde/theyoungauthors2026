<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A devious kitty – An Ode to Macavity</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>by Marc Auf der Heyde</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I know a kitty, so cute and full of life,<br/>
I know a kitty with nails sharper than a knife,<br/>
This kitty has two eyes, that gleam gold and glitter in the sun,<br/>
This kitty sets her own pace everywhere, she knows she mustn’t run,<br/>
And when this kitty gets a little bored, that’s when this kitty unveils her fun.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>You see this kitty, as cute as she may be,<br/>
Is a kitty sharp and witty, though not all can see,<br/>
She’s devious, she likes the game, she’ll take you for a ride,<br/>
And at the end of the night when it’s all done and gone, she’ll scratch you on your side.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>When she leaves you, and you open your eyes to find,<br/>
You’ll realize that this kitty has hit the road, leaving you a little blind.<br/>
You might feel strange, was there not just a kitty?<br/>
But this kitty is gone, this kitty is kind of shitty,<br/>
And so, you’ll jump up, try and form your legs,<br/>
You’ll chase after that kitty and scrape up her dregs,<br/>
Up up and down the rabbit hole, I’m looking for a kitten,<br/>
If I don’t find this feline character I think I’ll be quite smitten,<br/>
She’s taken a left, maybe a right,<br/>
Could have also gone up or down though, are you sure this is worth the fight?</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>And that’s just it, the question, that lingers when you give it in,<br/>
Is this devious little kitten, really worth this blasphemous sin,<br/>
Surely the time could be spent otherwise, not looking for this kitten,<br/>
But the fascination pulls you in, she is the antivenom and you have been bitten,<br/>
You need to catch this kitty, to learn her devious ways, <br/>
Learn how she can come and go, unmoved by the changing days,<br/>
Her wisdom will fulfil you, in healthy portions as knowledge goes,<br/>
For even lessons from a kitten can overwhelm and step on your toes.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>In a funny way this kitty never changes, this kitty never leaves,<br/>
But if you get close to catching this kitty, through your fingers she will flee,<br/>
She is not meant to be caught, she cannot be so easily tamed,<br/>
So we continue chasing for the thrill, to catch what is so dearly famed,<br/>
The devious kitty, who wants to be chased,<br/>
The devious kitty, oh what is this place?</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
