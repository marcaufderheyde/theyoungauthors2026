<b>She told me. Long sentences. </b>

They all felt cold, likes blades against my skin,

My <strong>mind has changed</strong> she says, blinking twice in confirmation,

I stare in sin,

I loved and love, and will love on,

How <strong>I learn now, here</strong> when you know, what went where

Clear perhaps, clean maybe even,

My mind, but not my soul.

<em>by anonymous</em>
